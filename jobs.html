    Would you like to teach web development with Deep Dive Coders?

    We’d love to have you. We’re looking for women and men with a passion for computers and web development and for working closely with others. Please tell us a little about yourself and your experience:

    1) Why do you want to teach web development and PHP with Deep Dive Coders?

    2) What have you been working on lately? What programming work have you been doing?

    3) Do you have previous experience teaching? If so, please describe it.

    4) Have you worked as a project lead or member of a project team? If so, please describe your role(s) and how you collaborated with your colleagues to ensure overall success of the project(s) in a paragraph or so.

    5) How do you describe your familiarity and experience with the gamut of modern software and web development, including the aspects below?

    HTML5

    CSS3

    UX / UI / Design

    Databases

    LAMP / WAMP / MAMP stacks

    PHP

    GitHub and/or other version control

    Cross-browser functionality

    Challenges of teaching a group that potentially uses Linux, Windows, and Mac machines in the same cohort

    Project-based work, with inherent real-world applications

    CMS such as WordPress, Drupal, Joomla

    6) How will you use your experience to teach others the basics? Your students will potentially come to your with little programming experience besides some required online prework they will complete prior to beginning the on-ground course.

    Please email us with your responses and your resume, portfolios, and other profiles and information you think will be relevant.

    hello@deepdivecoders.com

    We appreciate your interest and we’ll be in touch shortly.
