Who Works With Us

ABQ WebGeeks

ABQ Web Geeks was born of a local drink up, standing on the shoulders of previous presentation groups. We are dedicated to supporting the growth of web professionals in the broader Albuquerque area. We strive to promote knowledge across all facets of web creation while embracing the open nature of the medium. We are of the community, by the community, and for the community. Please join us as we grow a better Internet and a better Albuquerque.

Startup ABQ

Led by Entrepreneurs, For Entrepreneurs, StartupABQ was created to spur new company creation and entrepreneurial network density in Albuquerque. Our goal is to help create a vibrant and collaborative startup environment in order to strengthen the Albuquerque business community.

StartupNM

Startup New Mexico's mission is to foster vibrant entrepreneurism across the Land of Enchantment. Startup New Mexico is a statewide effort led by entrepreneurs to support the growth of startups throughout New Mexico, and is the latest Startup Region collaborating with the Startup America Partnership, a national movement focused on providing startups access to the investors, services, corporations and customers that help young companies grow and create jobs.

Startup Teen

StartupTeen is dedicated to providing information and education to young entrepreneurs, nerds and tech enthusiasts.

JohnnyBoards

JohnnyBoards places indoor advertising in community centers, hotels, and concert venues all over New Mexico. Johnny Boards can be seen in restrooms, elevators, by water fountains, and mail box areas around the state in locations such as health clubs, restaurants, golf courses, and hotels. With Johnny Boards, your audience is truly captive for 30 seconds to as much as 4 minutes.

TedxAbq

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxABQ, where x = independently organized Ted event. At our TEDx ABQ event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to apart deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
