Program Overview

Deep Dive Coders is a web development and programming bootcamp located in the historic, convenient and trendy EDo or East Downtown neighborhood in Albuquerque, NM. At Deep Dive Coders you'll become a web developer in just 2-1/2 months so you can get a great job as a programmer.

We're right on Historic Route 66, located in a beautiful and comfortable space, with many great restaurants, bars, and shops in easy walking distance. We're less than a 5-minute drive from the University of New Mexico, Downtown, I-25 and I-40, with easy access to and from anywhere else you need to be.

Here's what you'll learn:

Initial Phase - Introduction and Discovery

Weeks 1-2: During the first couple of weeks you'll learn to talk and work as a hacker, how all the different parts work together to make software run, and become familiar with the AMP stack. You'll learn to use a variety of development tools and you'll start building your own stuff.

Second Phase - Preparation and Development

Weeks 3-5: The reasons for learning a variety of tools and skills the initial few weeks will become clearer as you modify existing code and build your own through pair coding and your individual work. The gloves come off as you take projects from ideas, to bits and bytes, to functioning on the web - just as you always pictured it happening. Your fingers have now become one with your laptop.

Since your goal is to get a programming job, some of your best projects going forward will be included in your portfolio. Your portfolio will enable employers to see the coding work you have done with your own two hands.

Final Phase - Execution and Deployment

Weeks 6-9: The home stretch is when you really deep dive into PHP coding and build out your portfolio for prospective employers. You'll be pleasantly surprised at how much it all makes sense to you now. You'll be creating clean, functional and documented code, which is exactly what employers are looking and paying for.

And now on to a great coding job!

We have in New Mexico an expanding network of employers who will need to hire web developers and software engineers, and they'll be taking a look at you. We'll continually promote you and your coding skills, as well as the strong work ethic you've demonstrated during your time at Deep Dive Coders. Of course you are responsible for getting your job in the end, but we'll do all we can to help.

Discounts / Scholarships

We're committed to helping build a diverse tech community. If you're a veteran, a woman, or belong to another underrepresented group (Latino, Native American, Pacific Islander, African American) let us know and you'll be eligible to receive a $500 scholarship.

If you accept and work at least 100 days at a full-time job with one of our partner companies, we'll refund up to $1000 of your tuition.
